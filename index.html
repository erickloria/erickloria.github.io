About Me

👨‍🏫
Erick Loría Soto
Physics & TOK Teacher · UWC Costa Rica

I was born in Costa Rica but raised in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, which has given me a unique blend of cultures and experiences that I bring to my teaching and everyday life.

As a passionate educator and lifelong learner, I've dedicated my career to teaching Physics, Astronomy, and various other courses. My goal is to create engaging and dynamic lessons that spark curiosity and foster deep understanding in my students.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Physics from the Universidad de Costa Rica (UCR) and am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Renewable Energy and Energy Projects from CEUPE – Centro Europeo de Posgrado y Empresa. I'm also working towards my yoga teacher certification.

Outside the classroom, I have a deep passion for astronomy and technology. I love to travel and connect with nature through hiking, finding inspiration in the great outdoors. In my time off, I help at a yoga retreat center in the Osa Peninsula, combining my love for teaching and yoga in a beautiful natural setting.
