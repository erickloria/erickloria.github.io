Meet Erick Loria Soto

I was born in Costa Rica but raised in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, which has given me a blend of cultures and experiences that I bring to my teaching and everyday life.

As an educator and lifelong learner, I have dedicated my career to teaching Physics, Astronomy, and related courses. My goal is to design learning experiences that are engaging, rigorous, and intellectually curious.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Physics from the Universidad de Costa Rica and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Teaching in the Natural Sciences at Universidad Nacional in Costa Rica.

Outside the classroom, I have a deep interest in astronomy and technology. I enjoy travel, hiking, and time in nature, and I also help at a yoga retreat center in the Osa Peninsula.
