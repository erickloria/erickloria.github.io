About the Physics EE

The Extended Essay in Physics is a 4,000-word independent research paper. It should present a focused research question investigated through primary data collection or rigorous secondary source analysis. It is assessed on: focus, argument, structure, critical thinking, and formal presentation.

📝 Your EE supervisor will provide personalised deadlines and feedback. Add your individual planning documents and drafts to Google Drive and link them here.
