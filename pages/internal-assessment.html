About the Physics IA

The Internal Assessment is a 10-hour practical investigation worth 20% of your final IB score. It is assessed on five criteria: Personal Engagement, Exploration, Analysis, Evaluation, and Communication. The final report is typically 6–12 pages.

📋 IA topic proposals are due at the start of Year 2. Individual feedback and draft submissions will be managed through Google Classroom.
