About the Physics IA

The Internal Assessment is a 10-hour practical investigation worth 20% of your final IB score. It is assessed on five criteria: Personal Engagement, Exploration, Analysis, Evaluation, and Communication. The final report has a 3,000-word limit.

Note IA topic proposals are due at the start of Year 2. Use the structure guide and examples below to understand the expected organization and level of analysis.
