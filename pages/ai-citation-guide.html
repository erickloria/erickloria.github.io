Institutional Integrity Policy

Cite AI whenever it contributes meaningfully to your final work, and remember that the assignment level declared by the teacher determines whether AI use is permitted at all. If a teacher gives a required format, that format overrides the general guide.

APA 7th Edition

Use author-date format and cite either a specific AI chat or the AI tool generally, depending on what the reader needs to retrieve.

OpenAI. (2025). ChatGPT [Large language model]. https://chatgpt.com/
  • check_circle Describe prompts in the method note, appendix, or disclosure if relevant.
  • check_circle Use the exact model name shown in your session when available.
history_edu

MLA 9th Edition

Do not treat the AI tool as the author. Use the generated output or prompt title as the source title and the AI tool as the container.

"Describe the theme of nature in Jane Austen's Mansfield Park" prompt. ChatGPT, model GPT-4o, OpenAI, 23 Sept. 2024, chatgpt.com/share/...
  • check_circle Cite the specific prompt or output used.
  • check_circle Add a brief note if AI support was substantive.

Chicago Style

NOTES & BIBLIOGRAPHY

Chicago usually credits AI-generated text in prose or notes. Bibliography entries are only appropriate when there is a stable public link.

1. Text generated by ChatGPT, OpenAI, [Day Mon. Year], https://chat.openai.com/chat.

Author

Usually the company or provider.

Date

Use the specific generation date whenever possible.

Stability

Only add bibliography-style entries when the interaction has a stable public URL.

NotebookLM

NotebookLM responses need two layers of attribution: the NotebookLM interaction itself and the original documents uploaded into the notebook.

  • check_circle Cite the NotebookLM interaction when relevant.
  • check_circle Cite the underlying readings or notes separately.
